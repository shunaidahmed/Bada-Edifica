## About Us

Bada Edifica is a construction and building company based in Barcelona, Spain. We were founded in 2023 with one simple goal: to handle every part of a building project under one roof, with quality and honesty at the heart of everything we do.

Although we are a young company, our team has years of real, hands-on experience in electrical work, plumbing, construction, renovation, site management, and fire safety. Our clients get the dependability of experienced professionals, with the energy of a company that is still growing.

## Our Story

Bada Edifica started because we saw a common problem in the construction industry — clients were being let down by poor communication, broken promises, and too many different contractors who did not work well together.

We decided to build something better: one team, covering every trade, working together from start to finish.

We began with home and small commercial projects in Barcelona and quickly built a name for delivering on our word.

### Our Journey

**2023 — Founded**
Launched in Barcelona with a core team covering electrical, plumbing, and construction work.

**2023 — First Projects Done**
Completed our first round of home renovation and electrical projects, earning strong referrals and five-star feedback from clients.

**2024 — Fire Safety Added**
Expanded our services to include fire detection and suppression systems, fully certified for life-safety installations.

**2024 — Commercial Work Began**
Started taking on commercial projects — offices, shops, and mixed-use buildings.

**2025 — Growing Across Catalonia**
With a solid portfolio and a trusted reputation, we continue to take on bigger projects across the region.
